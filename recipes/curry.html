Japanese Curry

Description

This is my first attempt at a basic japanese curry in college, with it being the third iteration after the first was too bland and the second was too salty.

I will slowly make improvements on it over the course of the semester of course.

Ingredients

  • White/Yellow Onion
  • Carrot
  • Yellow/ Waxy Potato
  • Oil
  • Beef cuts
  • Butter
  • Chicken stock
  • Curry roux (Java or Vermont or Golden Curry)
  • Cumin
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Apples
  • Butter
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Chop onions, chunk carrots and potatoes, grate apples
  2. Season beef with salt and pepper, cumin, five spice.
  3. Beef into hot oil, stir until browned.
  4. Then onions, stir until browned or caramelized
  5. Then throw in carrots, potatoes, grated apples, onions
  6. Stir until mixed well, season with salt, pepper, cumin
  7. Fill with chicken stock until barely covered, heat to low simmer
  8. Cook until potatoes softened, then add curry roux
  9. Stir until good consistency
  10. Then finish with butter and soy sauce
