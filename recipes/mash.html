Description

This is my theoretical recipe for mashed potato in college, having not tested it at all. I will attempt it the weekend of 9/7 - 9/8 in 2024.

I will slowly make improvements on it over the course of the semester.

Ingredients

  • Yukon Gold Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Green scallions

Steps

  1. Peel, chunk smally, and into cold water they go
  2. Salt, then bring to simmer
  3. Cook 15-20, until easily poked
  4. Drain, mash, then cold milk and melted butter and mix thoroughly
  5. Salt and pepper to taste, then maybe cayenne pepper and scallions
